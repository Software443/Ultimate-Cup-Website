About Ultimate Cup

The Ultimate Cup is a grassroots football tournament by Red Spinel, created to engage amateur clubs in Lafia, Nasarawa State. It promotes community development and youth empowerment by offering a platform for local teams to compete and grow.

🎯 Our Mission

To use sports as a powerful tool for mental, physical, and academic development—helping young people grow stronger and more confident.

🚀 Our Vision

To empower young athletes to unlock their potential, become self-reliant, and rise to represent Nasarawa State on global stages.
